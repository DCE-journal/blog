Data-Centric Engineering Blog

This is the blog for Data-Centric Engineering (cambridge.org/dce), an open-access journal at the interface of engineering and data science. Editors in chief: Prof. Mark Girolami (University of Cambridge & The Alan Turing Institute); Eleni Chatzi (ETH Zürich); Kenichi Soga (University of California Berkeley). Published by CUP.
